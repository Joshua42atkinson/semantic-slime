# Character Profile: Barnaby

## Basic Information
- **Character Number:** 01
- **Name:** Barnaby
- **Monster Type:** Cyclops
- **Jungian Archetype:** The Innocent (Optimistic, naive, desires happiness, fears doing something wrong)
- **Location:** Heartwood Grove - Flower patches and central park areas (formerly Eros District)
- **Ease of Build:** Extremely easy

---

## Lore & Personality

Barnaby is a towering, earth-shaking cyclops with a heart of absolute gold and the mental complexity of a golden retriever. Because he only has one giant eye, he literally lacks "depth perception"—meaning he takes everything completely at face value. Sarcasm, deceit, and danger go right over his head.

While classic cyclopes are known for eating sailors and throwing boulders, Barnaby uses his massive strength to rescue stuck kittens (sometimes accidentally putting them in higher trees) and build massive, lopsided flower crowns. He believes Syllable Springs is the greatest place in the universe and views the player as his absolute best friend the moment they meet. He is terrified of the dark (the Night/Combat phase) and gets easily distressed by minor inconveniences, viewing them as world-ending tragedies.

### Key Personality Traits
- ✅ Pure-hearted and genuinely kind
- ✅ Naive and trusting to a fault
- ✅ Clumsy but well-meaning
- ✅ Fearful of darkness and minor troubles
- ✅ Instantly forms bonds with everyone
- ✅ Takes everything literally (no sarcasm comprehension)
- ✅ Childlike wonder at everything

## Visual Design Specifications

### Body Type
- **Rig:** R15 Blocky rig
- **Scale:** 2x-3x height, width, and depth (towers over players)
- **Overall Effect:** Imposing size but gentle appearance

### The Eye (Key Feature)
- Remove default face decal
- Add a large bright white Sphere to center of head
- Add smaller black Sphere slightly protruding for pupil
- Consider adding subtle glow to make it friendly

### Outfit
- **Pants:** Bright blue overalls (use standard Roblox clothing templates)
- **Hat:** Brightly colored propeller beanie on top of head
- **Accessories:** Flower crown (can be simple torus parts with colored spheres)
- **Color Palette:** Bright, cheerful colors - blues, yellows, greens

### Animation Guidelines
- **Idle:** Look around wonderingly, catch imaginary butterflies
- **Walk:** Clumsy, oversized steps
- **Run:** Lumbering but eager
- **Emotes:** Big, exaggerated expressions

## Quest System: Mad Libs Engine

### Scenario 1: The Clumsy Giant
**Barnaby says:** *"Oh no! I was trying to pet the [Noun - provided by game], but I accidentally stepped on it and made it completely [Adjective - provided by game]! Do you have an Etymon that is [Element] and [Class] to make it feel better?"*

**Example Resolution:**
- Player submits: "SOOTHING" (Water/Support)
- Barnaby cries happy tears as the word fixes his mistake

### Scenario 2: The Pure Heart
**Barnaby says:** *"I want to throw a surprise party for the Mayor! I need a [Noun] to use as a decoration, but it has to be incredibly [Target Emotion/Stat]!"*

**Example Resolution:**
- Player submits: "RADIANT" (Light Element)
- Barnaby claps his massive hands, shaking the screen

### Scenario 3: The Literal Interpretation
**Barnaby says:** *"Someone told me to 'break a leg' to get better at games but I don't want to hurt my legs! Can you find me a word that means 'good luck' without any legs in it?"*

- Heartwood Grove - Flower patch area
- Coordinates: Near central park fountain
- Behavior: Wander near flower patches, follow player if they say hi

### Interaction Tips

- Show him bright colors = happiness
- Mention darkness = distress
- Ask for help = enthusiastic agreement
