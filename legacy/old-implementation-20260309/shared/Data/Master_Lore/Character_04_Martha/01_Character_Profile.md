# Character Profile: Martha

## Basic Information
- **Character Number:** 04
- **Name:** Martha
- **Monster Type:** Gargoyle
- **Jungian Archetype:** The Caregiver (Maternal, protective, compassionate, desires to protect others from harm, fears selfishness and ingratitude)
- **Location:** Whisper Winds - Perched on low stone wall near fountains (formerly Pneuma District)
- **Ease of Build:** Moderate

---

## Lore & Personality

Martha was carved to be a terrifying, demon-warding guardian of the town walls, but her magical animation process infused her with the personality of an overbearing, loving grandmother. She is made of solid, unyielding granite, yet she spends her days fussing over the health and wellbeing of Syllable Springs's residents.

She constantly worries that everyone is too skinny, not wearing enough layers, or pushing themselves too hard. She tries to feed adventurers her famous "granite soup" (which is literally just hot water and gravel) and will aggressively knit ethereal sweaters out of spare vowels. Despite her terrifying fangs and bat-like wings, she is the emotional anchor of the town. She doesn't fight off evil spirits with claws; she shoos them away with a broom and a stern lecture about their manners.

### Key Personality Traits
- ✅ Maternal and protective
- ✅ Constantly worries about others
- ✅ Feeds everyone (even if food is questionable)
- ✅ Aggressively knits for people
- ✅ Soft heart beneath stone exterior
- ✅ Fusses over minor health concerns
- ✅ Shoos away evil with broom and lectures

## Quest System: Mad Libs Engine

### Scenario 1: The Magical Sniffles
**Martha says:** *"Oh, dear! Poor Pip the Slime was playing in the [Cold Weather Noun] and now he has a terrible case of the [Silly Disease]! I need to make a broth, but I'm missing the main ingredient. Please bring me an Etymon of pure [Element] and [Class] to warm his little gelatinous core!"*

**Example Resolution:**
- Player submits: "WARMING" (Fire/Support)
- Martha beams: "Perfect! He'll be back to bouncing in no time. Did you want a bowl for yourself, dear?"

### Scenario 2: The Preventive Sweater
**Martha says:** *"The wind from the [Location] is picking up! If we don't bundle up the [Group of People], they'll catch their death! Help me knit a barrier! I need an Etymon with high [Target Stat] to stitch into this scarf!"*

**Example Resolution:**
- Player submits: "WHOLESOME" (High HP)
- Martha wraps an invisible, cozy scarf around you: "There now, nice and snug! Don't stay out too late!"

### Scenario 3: The Imaginary Illness
**Martha says:** *"Ohh, you don't look so good, dear. Your letters are looking a bit droopy. Let me make you some soup. I need a word that's [Element] and [Class] to restore your energy!"*
