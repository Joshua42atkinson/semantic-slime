---

## Lore & Personality

In standard fantasy, goblins hoard gold and shiny trinkets out of sheer greed. Gribble, however, hoards information. He is an overly caffeinated, self-proclaimed archaeologist and cartographer who cannot sit still for more than three seconds. He maps everything, including the inside of the local tavern's pantry, which he insists is a "labyrinth of fermented grains."

Gribble is absolutely fearless because his curiosity completely overrides his sense of self-preservation. He doesn't want to conquer the world; he just wants to know what happens if you pull that weird lever over there. He views the player as a fellow expeditionary partner and is constantly dragging them into highly enthusiastic, poorly planned schemes to uncover the "deepest secrets" of Syllable Springs (which usually just involves finding where Yorick hid the spare broom).

### Key Personality Traits
- ✅ Hyperactive and jittery
- ✅ Obsessively curious about everything
- ✅ Self-proclaimed archaeologist/cartographer
- ✅ Fearless (curiosity overrides self-preservation)
- ✅ Maps mundane things as "ancient ruins"
- ✅ Enthusiastic but poorly planned schemes
- ✅ Hoards information, not gold

## Quest System: Mad Libs Engine

### Scenario 1: The Unyielding Door
**Gribble says:** *"Eureka! I've found the hidden entrance to the [Ancient Location]! But the mechanism is blocked by a giant [Gross Noun]! I need an explosive linguistic catalyst! Bring me an Etymon of pure [Element] and [Class] to blow it wide open!"*

**Example Resolution:**
- Player submits: "VOLATILE" (Fire/Striker)
- Gribble cheers: "Brilliant! Now, let's see what's in—oh, it's just a linen closet. The plot thickens!"

### Scenario 2: The Dark Chasm
**Gribble says:** *"My map says the legendary [Shiny Object] is just past this corridor, but it's completely pitch black and filled with [Plural Noun]! We need illumination! Craft me an Etymon with high [Target Stat] to light the way and reveal the truth!"*

**Example Resolution:**
- Player submits: "CLARITY" (Light/High Accuracy)
- Gribble adjusts goggles: "Ah! I see it! Wait, that's just a shiny rock. I'm bagging it anyway!"

### Scenario 3: The Ancient Artifact
**Gribble says:** *"I've uncovered an ancient artifact! It appears to be... a rusty spoon? But what secrets does it HOLD?! I need a word of [Element] and [Class] to properly analyze it!"*
