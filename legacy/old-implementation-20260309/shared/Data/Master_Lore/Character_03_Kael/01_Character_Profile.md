# Character Profile: Kael

## Basic Information
- **Character Number:** 03
- **Name:** Kael
- **Monster Type:** Minotaur
- **Jungian Archetype:** The Hero (Courageous, seeks to prove worth through valiant action, desires to protect the weak, fears being seen as a monster or being too weak to save the day)
- **Location:** Town Hub - Guarding near the Slime Synthesizer (self-appointed "Grand Paladin of the Fabricator")
- **Ease of Build:** Moderate/Easy

---

## Lore & Personality

Kael is an eight-foot-tall wall of muscle, fur, and horns who looks like a terrifying end-game boss but possesses the heart of a chivalrous knight. He is entirely obsessed with valor, honor, and the classic "Hero's Journey." However, because he is a massive Minotaur, he is constantly terrified of his own strength. He tiptoes around the town so he doesn't crush the flowers and speaks in a booming, dramatic voice that he constantly tries (and fails) to whisper with.

Kael desperately wants to slay dragons and rescue royalty, but Syllable Springs is generally pretty safe. Therefore, he heavily romanticizes and dramatically overstates minor issues. A lost cat is a "hostage of the shadows," and a broken fence is a "breach in the fortress walls." He views the player as a fellow squire or knight-errant and takes his role as a quest-giver incredibly seriously.

### Key Personality Traits
- ✅ Chivalrous and honorable to a fault
- ✅ Terrified of his own strength
- ✅ Dramatic and romanticizes everything
- ✅ Deeply cares about being seen as a hero (not a monster)
- ✅ Tries to whisper but fails spectacularly
- ✅ Tiptoes everywhere (ironic for his size)
- ✅ Takes quest-giving incredibly seriously

## Quest System: Mad Libs Engine

### Scenario 1: The Fearsome Beast
**Kael says:** *"Hail, champion! A terrifying [Small Animal] has laid siege to the [Location]! It threatens to destroy our morale! We must banish it gently! I need an Etymon of pure [Element] and [Class] to whisk the beast away without harming a hair on its head!"*

**Example Resolution:**
- Player submits: "GENTLE" (Air/Support)
- Kael salutes: "A flawless victory! The beast has been vanquished to a nice farm upstate!"

### Scenario 2: The Broken Aegis
**Kael says:** *"Disaster strikes! The great barrier—uh, I mean, the [Mundane Object]—has been shattered by the wind! If we do not mend it, the [Plural Noun] will run amok! Bestow upon me a word of high [Target Stat] to restore our defenses!"*

**Example Resolution:**
- Player submits: "FORTIFY" (Earth/High Defense)
- Kael flexes proudly: "The realm is secure once more, thanks to your linguistic valor!"

### Scenario 3: The Dragon (Actually a Lizard)
**Kael says:** *"BEHOLD! A DRAGON approaches! Wait... that's just Gary the garden lizard. But what if he BECOMES a dragon?! We must prepare! Give me a word of [Element] to protect us from draconic menace!"*

## Implementation Notes

### Scripting Priorities
1. **Dramatic Dialogue System:** Over-the-top, theatrical speech
2. **Quest Giver:** Initialize quests at Dusk phase
3. **Animation Controller:** Heroic poses, dramatic freezes
4. **Strength Anxiety:** Comments about being careful not to break things

### Spawn Location
- Town Hub - Near Slime Synthesizer/crafting area
- Coordinates: Central area
- Behavior: Stand guard, patrol, give quests

### Interaction Tips
- Compliment his valor = extremely happy
- Mention his size = gets self-conscious
- Ask for quest = dramatically presents it
- Small creature nearby = freezes in terror of hurting it
- Night = becomes extra dramatic about "combat"
