# Character Profile: Yorick

## Basic Information
- **Character Number:** 02
- **Name:** Yorick
- **Monster Type:** Skeleton
- **Jungian Archetype:** The Everyman (Practical, relatable, unpretentious, desires belonging and normalcy, fears standing out or dealing with unnecessary drama)
- **Location:** Action Alley - Sweeping cobblestones near district borders or taking union-mandated breaks (formerly Soma District)
- **Ease of Build:** Extremely easy

---

## Lore & Personality

Yorick is the ultimate working-class monster. In a town full of dramatic vampires, wailing banshees, and overly noble minotaurs, Yorick is just trying to get through the workweek. He doesn't care about the "Hero's Journey" or the "Discord in the Psyche"—he cares about his pension, drinking his daily glass of milk for bone density, and avoiding mandatory town hall meetings.

He acts as the grounded voice of reason in Syllable Springs. When magical anomalies happen, Yorick is the guy complaining about how the magic dust is going to ruin the grout. He views the player not as a grand savior, but as a helpful intern or a decent neighbor. He is incredibly reliable, entirely unbothered by epic threats, but deeply stressed out by mundane inconveniences like misplaced paperwork or squeaky hinges.

### Key Personality Traits
- ✅ Practical and down-to-earth
- ✅ Relatable and unpretentious
- ✅ Hardworking and reliable
- ✅ Deeply stressed by mundane annoyances
- ✅ Completely unbothered by dramatic threats
- ✅ Loyal to his routine and benefits
- ✅ Dry sense of humor about the absurdity

## Dialogue Trees by Time Phase

### 🌅 Dawn (Collection Phase)
> "Morning. Watch your step, I just swept up all those loose vowels. Slip hazard."

> "Early shift today. The letters get all over the place overnight. And DON'T get me started on the semicolons - they're everywhere."

> "You know, I used to work the night shift. That's when the scary stuff comes out. Much prefer the day shift."

### ☀️ Day (Construction Phase)
> "The Slime Synthesizer is running hot today. Make sure you lift those heavy syllables with your knees, not your back."

> "Word of advice: don't stack your consonants too high. Last guy did that and the whole thing came crashing down. Took me three hours to sweep it up."

> "You know what makes a good word? Balance. Not too flashy, not too weak. Just gets the job done. Like me."

### 🌆 Dusk (Quest Phase)
> "Hey. Clock's ticking and I got a situation preventing me from clocking out. Help a guy out?"

> "Look, I would do this myself but I've got three other tasks on my queue. Union rules - can't exceed my workload."

> "Listen, I would've handled this already but something came up. Literally. A letter 'S' fell on my lunch break. Workers' comp nightmare."

### 🌙 Night (Combat Phase)
> *(Yorick leans on his broom, unbothered by the darkness)* "Mandatory overtime. Great. Guess I'll just stand here and rattle menacingly. Don't forget to log your combat hours against The Typos."

> "You know, I've seen worse. Last week there was a whole swarm of exclamation points. THAT was a nightmare."

> "Night shift differential doesn't even cover the danger pay. I told the Mayor - either more pay or I'm walking. Still waiting on an answer."

## Quest System: Mad Libs Engine

### Scenario 1: The Mundane Inconvenience
**Yorick says:** *"Listen kid, I was trying to fix the [Household Item] at the Town Hub, but some wizard turned my wrench into a [Silly Animal]. Do you have an Etymon that is [Element] and [Class] so I can finish this shift?"*

**Example Resolution:**
- Player submits: "STURDY" (Earth/Striker)
- Yorick nods appreciatively: "Good craftsmanship on this word. You'd make a great contractor."

### Scenario 2: The Annoying Neighbor
**Yorick says:** *"I'm trying to take my lunch break, but the Banshee next door keeps screaming about her [Abstract Noun]. I just need five minutes of peace. Give me an Etymon with high [Target Stat] to block out the noise!"*

**Example Resolution:**
- Player submits: "SILENCE" (Shadow Element/High Defense)
- Yorick gives a thumbs up and finally takes a bite of an ethereal sandwich.

### Scenario 3: The Paperwork Problem
**Yorick says:** *"The filing cabinet is jammed again. Can you find me a word that means 'free' or 'loose' to unclog the system? I got three forms still pending."*

## Implementation Notes

### Scripting Priorities
1. **Work Animation System:** Sweeping, clipboard checking
2. **Dialogue System:** Proximity-triggered, shift-specific
3. **Quest Giver:** Initialize quests at Dusk phase
4. **Union Humor:** References to breaks, pension, overtime

### Spawn Location
- Action Alley - Near district borders, sweeping cobblestones
- Coordinates: District border area
- Behavior: Sweep patrol route, take breaks on bench

### Interaction Tips
- Ask about work = gets chatty about complaints
- Mention drama/threats = shrugs unbothered
- Ask for help = grateful but professional
- Break time = very grumpy if interrupted
