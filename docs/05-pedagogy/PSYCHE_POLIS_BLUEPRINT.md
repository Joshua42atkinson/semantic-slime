## 4. The Logos Engine: Word-Creature Metaphysics
**Etymons** are the collectible entities. Not "Pokemon," but "Living Words."

### 4.1 Ontology of an Etymon
Based on **Roots** (Elemental Soul) and **Suffixes** (Body/Class).

#### Roots (Element):
- **Ignis (Fire):** Heat, passion. *High Attack.*
- **Aqua (Water):** Flow, emotion. *High Health.*
- **Terra (Earth):** Structure, stability. *High Defense.*
- **Aer (Air):** Intellect, speed. *High Speed/Evasion.*
- **Umbra (Shadow):** Hidden, fear. *Debuffs/Stealth.*
- **Lux (Light):** Truth, vision. *Buffs/Reveal.*

#### Suffixes (Morphology/Class):
- **-tion (Noun):** Concrete. *Tank/Bruiser*. (e.g., Ignition = Fire Golem)
- **-ize (Verb):** Action. *Striker/Caster*. (e.g., Liquefy = Water Assassin)
- **-ous (Adjective):** Quality. *Support/Buffer*. (e.g., Structural = Earth Buffer)

### 4.2 Collection: Etymological Excavation
1.  **Encounter:** A "Vague Concept" (shimmering cloud).
2.  **Riddle:** It whispers a definition.
3.  **Casting:** Player types the word.
4.  **Analysis:** Player "slices" the word (Root + Suffix) to stabilize it.

### 4.3 Combat: Speech Act System (Dialectic Duel)
Fighting is persuasion/transformation.
- **Assertives (Attack):** Facts. Logic damage.
- **Directives (Debuff):** Commands. Lower will.
- **Expressives (Heal):** Emotions. Restore sanity.
- **Syntactical Chaining:** Combo words into sentences for massive effects (e.g., [Verb: Protect] + [Object: Us] = Shield).
